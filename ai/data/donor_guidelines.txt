BLOODBRIDGE DONOR GUIDELINES

A donor should be between 18 and 65 years old.

The minimum weight required for blood donation is 50 kilograms.

A donor must wait 56 days between whole blood donations.

Individuals who recently had a tattoo should wait 6 months before donating blood.

Individuals who recently underwent major surgery should wait 12 months before donating blood.

Blood donation appointments in BloodBridge can be scheduled between 8 AM and 6 PM.

Donors earn BloodHero points after every successful donation.

A donor who completes 10 successful donations receives the Silver BloodHero badge.

A donor who completes 25 successful donations receives the Gold BloodHero badge.

BloodBridge recommends drinking at least 500 ml of water before donation.
